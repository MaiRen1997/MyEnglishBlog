口语中的变音

# 口语中的变音

# t + j -> tʃ

  1. last year
  2. don’t you
  3. won’t you
  4. statue
  5. situation

# s + j -> ʃ

  1. this year
  2. sexual
  3. Bless you.
  4. I promise you.

# d + j -> dʒ

  1. would you
  2. should you
  3. I need your help.

# z + j = ʒ

  1. use your book
  2. How’s your father
