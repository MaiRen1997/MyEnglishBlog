# escort

# 释义

  1. n. 陪同者；护送者
  2. vt. 护送；陪伴

# 例句

  1. Parents can't escort their child forever, therefore teaching them how to survive in society is more important. 父母不能永远护卫着孩子，因此教会他们如何在社会中生存更为重要。
  2. She hired an escort to accompany her to the formal event. 她雇了一名陪同者陪她参加正式活动。
  3. The police provided an escort for the visiting diplomat. 警方为来访的外交官提供了护送。

# eternal

# 释义

adj. 永恒的；永久的

adj. 似乎永远持续的；无休止的

# 例句

Many people believe in eternal life after death. 许多人相信死后有永恒的生命。

Their eternal love was celebrated in poems and songs. 他们永恒的爱情在诗歌和歌曲中被歌颂。

I'm tired of their eternal arguments; they never seem to stop. 我厌倦了他们无休止的争吵，他们似乎永远停不下来。

# bump

# 释义

n. 碰撞；撞击；肿块

vt. 碰撞；颠簸

vi. 颠簸行进；偶然遇到

# 例句

The car hit a bump in the road and everyone was jolted. 汽车撞到了路上的一个突起，所有人都被颠了一下。

She got a small bump on her head after bumping into the door. 她撞到门上后，头上起了个小包。

I often bump into old friends at the supermarket. 我经常在超市偶然遇到老朋友。

# faithful

# 释义

adj. 忠诚的；忠实的

adj. 准确的；如实的

# 例句

He has been a faithful friend to me for over twenty years. 他是我二十多年来忠实的朋友。

The movie is a faithful adaptation of the novel. 这部电影是对小说的忠实改编。

Dogs are known for being faithful companions. 狗以忠诚的伴侣而闻名。

# absurd

# 释义

adj. 荒谬的；荒唐的

# 例句

It is absurd to think that pigs can fly. 认为猪会飞是荒谬的。

The idea that the earth is flat is completely absurd. 地球是平的这种想法完全荒唐。

He made an absurd excuse for being late. 他为迟到编了一个荒唐的借口。

# shrink

# 释义

vi. 收缩；缩小

vt. 使收缩；使缩小

n. 心理医生（俚语）

# 例句

Wool sweaters often shrink if washed in hot water. 羊毛衫用热水洗经常会缩水。

The company had to shrink its workforce due to financial difficulties. 公司因财务困难不得不裁员。

He’s been seeing a shrink to deal with his anxiety. 他一直在看心理医生处理焦虑问题。

# reservation

# 释义

n. 预订；保留

n. 保留意见；疑虑

# 例句

I made a reservation for two at the restaurant. 我在餐厅预订了两人位。

She agreed to the plan with some reservations. 她带着一些保留意见同意了该计划。

The hotel confirmed our reservation by email. 酒店通过邮件确认了我们的预订。

# disgrace

# 释义

n. 耻辱；丢脸

vt. 使丢脸；使蒙羞

# 例句

His behavior was a disgrace to the family. 他的行为是家族的耻辱。

The scandal disgraced the entire organization. 这起丑闻令整个组织蒙羞。

It’s a disgrace that so many people are still living in poverty. 这么多人仍生活在贫困中，这是一种耻辱。

# bully

# 释义

n. 欺凌者；恶霸

vt. 欺负；威吓

# 例句

He was a bully in school who often picked on younger kids. 他在学校是个恶霸，经常欺负小孩子。

Don’t let anyone bully you into doing something you don’t want to. 不要让任何人威逼你做你不想做的事。

Workplace bullying can have serious effects on mental health. 职场欺凌会对心理健康产生严重影响。

# negotiate

# 释义

vi. 谈判；协商

vt. 谈判达成；顺利通过

# 例句

The two countries are negotiating a peace treaty. 两国正在谈判达成和平条约。

She successfully negotiated a higher salary. 她成功协商到了更高的薪水。

It’s important to negotiate the terms before signing a contract. 在签订合同前协商条款很重要。

# esteem

# 释义

n. 尊重；敬重

vt. 尊重；敬重

# 例句

He is held in high esteem by his colleagues. 他深受同事们的敬重。

Self-esteem is crucial for mental well-being. 自尊对心理健康至关重要。

She esteems her mentor for his wisdom and kindness. 她敬重她的导师，因为他智慧而善良。

# essence

# 释义

n. 本质；精髓；香精

# 例句

The essence of good communication is listening. 良好沟通的本质在于倾听。

This perfume captures the essence of spring flowers. 这款香水捕捉了春花的精髓。

In essence, his argument was about fairness. 本质上，他的论点关乎公平。

# famine

# 释义

n. 饥荒；严重短缺

# 例句

The country suffered a severe famine last year. 该国去年遭受了严重的饥荒。

Many people died during the Great Famine in Ireland. 许多人在爱尔兰大饥荒期间死去。

There is a famine of good leadership in the organization. 该组织缺乏优秀的领导。

# norm

# 释义

n. 规范；标准；常态

# 例句

Working from home has become the new norm. 在家办公已成为新常态。

Social norms vary from culture to culture. 社会规范因文化而异。

It’s against the norm to interrupt someone while they’re speaking. 在别人说话时打断是不符合规范的。

# discourage

# 释义

vt. 使灰心；阻止；劝阻

# 例句

Don’t let failure discourage you from trying again. 不要让失败阻止你再次尝试。

High prices may discourage people from buying electric cars. 高价格可能会阻止人们购买电动汽车。

His negative attitude discouraged the whole team. 他的消极态度让整个团队感到灰心。

# pebble

# 释义

n. 鹅卵石；小圆石

# 例句

She picked up a smooth pebble from the beach. 她从海滩上捡起一块光滑的鹅卵石。

The path was paved with colorful pebbles. 这条小路铺满了彩色的鹅卵石。

A pebble in my shoe made walking uncomfortable. 我鞋里的小石子让我走路不舒服。

# skeptical

# 释义

adj. 怀疑的；持怀疑态度的

# 例句

Many people are skeptical about the effectiveness of the new policy. 许多人对新政策的有效性持怀疑态度。

She was skeptical of his promises until she saw results. 她对他的承诺持怀疑态度，直到看到结果。

Scientists remain skeptical until more evidence is provided. 科学家们持保留态度，直到有更多证据。

# disrupt

# 释义

vt. 扰乱；中断；破坏

# 例句

The protest disrupted traffic in the city center. 抗议活动扰乱了市中心的交通。

A sudden noise disrupted the meeting. 突然的噪音打断了会议。

Technology can disrupt traditional industries. 技术可能会颠覆传统行业。

# defer

# 释义

vt. 推迟；延期

vi. 遵从；听从

# 例句

The meeting has been deferred until next week. 会议已推迟到下周。

He deferred to his manager’s decision. 他遵从了经理的决定。

Students can defer their loans until after graduation. 学生可以延期还贷直到毕业后。

# offset

# 释义

vt. 抵消；弥补；补偿

# 例句

The company planted trees to offset its carbon emissions. 公司植树以抵消其碳排放。

Higher costs were offset by increased sales. 更高的成本被增加的销售额抵消了。

The benefits of the policy offset its drawbacks. 该政策的优点弥补了其缺点。

# bureaucracy

# 释义

n. 官僚制度；官僚主义

# 例句

Dealing with government bureaucracy can be time-consuming. 与政府官僚制度打交道可能很耗时。

The company’s bureaucracy slows down decision-making. 公司的官僚主义拖慢了决策速度。

He was frustrated by the bureaucracy involved in getting a visa. 他对办理签证涉及的官僚程序感到沮丧。

She acquainted herself with the local customs. 她熟悉了当地的风俗。

The training will acquaint employees with safety procedures. 培训将让员工熟悉安全程序。

# homo sapien

# 例句

Homo sapiens are the only surviving species of the genus Homo. 智人是人属中唯一现存的物种。

The evolution of Homo sapiens is a fascinating topic. 智人的进化是一个迷人的话题。

Homo sapiens first appeared in Africa around 300,000 years ago. 智人大约在30万年前首次出现在非洲。

# dwell

# 例句

Don’t dwell on past mistakes; learn from them. 不要老是想着过去的错误，要从中学习。

The book dwells on the themes of love and loss. 这本书详细论述了爱与失去的主题。

# acquaintance

# 释义

# 例句

He’s not a close friend, just an acquaintance. 他不是密友，只是个泛泛之交。

I have a slight acquaintance with French literature. 我对法国文学略知一二。

She made many acquaintances during her trip. 她在旅途中结识了许多人。

# reserve

# 释义

n. 储备；保留；自然保护区

# 例句

I’d like to reserve a table for four. 我想预订一张四人桌。

She spoke with reserve, choosing her words carefully. 她说话谨慎，措辞小心。

The wildlife reserve is home to many endangered species. 这个野生动物保护区是许多濒危物种的家园。

# mutual

# 释义

# 例句

Their relationship is based on mutual respect. 他们的关系建立在相互尊重的基础上。

The agreement benefits both parties in a mutual way. 该协议以互利的方式使双方受益。

# queer

# 释义

adj. 同性恋的（非正式，现多用LGBTQ+）

# 例句

There was a queer noise coming from the basement. 地下室传来一个奇怪的声音。

The word “queer” has been reclaimed by the LGBTQ+ community. “Queer”一词已被LGBTQ+群体重新使用。

He felt a queer sensation when entering the old house. 进入那栋老房子时，他有一种奇怪的感觉。

# survey

n. 调查；测量

# 例句

The survey shows that most people prefer working from home. 调查显示大多数人更喜欢在家工作。

They surveyed the land before building the house. 他们在建房子前测量了土地。

From the hill, you can survey the entire valley. 从山上可以俯瞰整个山谷。

# resolution

The high resolution of the screen makes images look sharp. 屏幕的高分辨率让图像看起来清晰。

# siege

# 例句

The city was under siege for three months. 这座城市被围困了三个月。

The siege ended when supplies ran out. 补给耗尽时围困结束了。

The castle survived many sieges throughout history. 这座城堡在历史上经历了多次围攻而幸存。

# stale

# 释义

adj. 不新鲜的；陈旧的；乏味的

# 例句

The bread is stale; we should buy a fresh loaf. 面包不新鲜了，我们应该买条新鲜的。

His jokes are stale and no one laughs anymore. 他的笑话老套，没人再笑了。

The air in the room felt stale and stuffy. 房间里的空气感觉闷热不流通。

# obedience

The soldiers acted in obedience to their commander’s orders. 士兵们服从指挥官的命令行动。

# tilt

There is a tilt towards renewable energy in recent policies. 最近的政策倾向于可再生能源。

# feudal

# 释义

The novel depicts life in a feudal society. 这部小说描绘了封建社会的生活。

# fluctuate

# 释义

Stock prices fluctuate daily. 股票价格每天都在波动。

# generalize

# 释义

She tends to generalize her opinions too broadly. 她倾向于把自己的观点泛化得太广。

# magnificent
