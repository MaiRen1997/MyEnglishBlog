Tue Nov 25 2025 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

# 单词1

# coordination

  1. These dancers have poor coordination. 这些舞蹈演员协调能力不好。
  2. Learning to drive is all a matter of coordination. 学开车主要是靠协调。

# redundant

  1. redundant labour 过剩的劳动力
  2. Half the workforce was redundant. 一半员工被解雇了。

# labour

  1. We labour hard to finish our job. 我们努力干活，以把工作干完。
  2. She was in labour for three hours. 她的分娩过程长达三小时。
  3. We labour for the happiness of mankind. 我们为人类的幸福而努力。

# workforce

  1. The whole workforce is on strike. 全体工作人员在罢工中。
  2. The workforce has not been paid for three weeks. 工人们已有三个星期未拿工钱了。
  3. A quarter of the local workforce is unemployed. 本地四分之一的职工都失业了。

# routine

  1. He made his routine trip to the store. 他依行惯例去商店。
  2. break the routine 打破常规
  3. Asking these questions is routine work. 问这些问题是例行公事。

# virtual

  1. A virtual state of war exists between the two countries. 这两国间实际上处于战争状态。
  2. This reply is a virtual acceptance of our offer. 这一回答几乎说明我们的建议被接受了。

# qualitative

  1. We can draw a few qualitative conclusions. 我们可以得出一些定性的结论。
  2. This will cause qualitative changes. 这将会引起质的变化。
  3. Qualitative methods are used in this study. 本研究采用了定性的研究方法。

# enemy

  1. He ought to know that it is the kings, not republicans, who are the monarchy's worst enemies. 他应该知道，君主制最大的敌人恰恰是君主，而不是共和党人。
  2. What political journalist, what party whip, would not want to know the makeup of the WhatsApp groups in which Theresa May's enemies are currently plotting? 特蕾莎•梅的政敌在WhatsApp群里密谋，哪些政治新闻记者和政党组织秘书会不想了解这些群的成员构成呢？

# arena

  1. the political arena 政治舞台
  2. The arena was crowded with audience. 竞技场中挤满了观众。
  3. the literary arena 文学界

# audience

  1. His speech won the applause of the audience. 他的讲话赢得了听众的掌声。
  2. The audience was amused by the performance. 观众被这个表演逗乐了。
  3. His long speech tired the audience. 他的长篇演讲令听众厌烦。

# amused

  1. The children were amused at the joke. 孩子们被笑话逗乐了。
  2. The boys amused themselves with games. 男孩们以游戏自娱。
  3. I amused myself by reading. 我读书以作消遣。

# eloquent

  1. The proof is eloquent. 这个证据是有说服力的。
  2. Her enthusiasm made her eloquent. 她的热情使她讲起话来口才流利。
  3. an eloquent speaker 一个雄辩的演讲家

# proof

  1. Have you got any proof that you own this bike? 你有什么证据证明这辆自行车是你的？
  2. This fact is too evident to require proof. 这事实很明显，用不着证明。
  3. absolute proof 确凿的证据

# confer

  1. I must confer with my lawyer before I decide. 我必须先同我的律师商量一下才能做出决定。
  2. The university will confer a master's degree on her. 学校将授予她硕士学位。
  3. to confer power on someone 赋予某人权力

# hypocrisy

  1. He is guilty of hypocrisy. 他有伪善之嫌。
  2. We were cheated by his hypocrisy. 我们都被他的伪善欺骗了。
  3. I can't put up with your hypocrisy any more. 我再也不能容忍你的虚伪态度了。

# magnitude

  1. I had not realized the magnitude of the problem. 我没意识到这个问题的重要性。
  2. The earthquake has a magnitude of 7.1. 这次地震的震级为7.1级。
  3. the magnitude of the universe 宇宙之广大

# upward

  1. Prices are tending upward. 物价在上涨。
  2. upward movement 向上的运动
  3. His eyes are looking straight upward. 他的眼睛直向上看。

# treadmill

  1. He runs on the treadmill every morning. 他每天早上都在跑步机上跑步。
  2. There is a treadmill in his room. 他房间里有一台跑步机。
  3. I'd like to escape the office treadmill. 我想摆脱办公室的枯燥工作。

# robust

  1. His robust body made him survive the disaster. 强壮的身体使他在灾难中幸存。
  2. That chair is not very robust. 那把椅子不太结实。
  3. She was almost 90, but still very robust. 她将近90岁了，但仍然很健壮。

# survive

  1. She was the only one to survive the air crash. 她是这次飞机失事的唯一幸存者。
  2. We cannot survive for long without food and drink. 我们没有食物和水就活不了多久。
  3. survive the war 在战争中存活下来

# disaster

They had led the country into economic disaster. 他们把国家带入了经济灾难中。

We should learn the lessons from this disaster. 我们要从这场灾难中汲取教训。

Wars only brought people disaster. 战争只会给人们带来灾难。

# adverse

  1. The development was adverse to our interests. 这种发展与我们的利益背道而驰。
  2. The drug has adverse side effects. 这种药物有有害的副作用。
  3. The judge gave us an adverse decision. 法官作出对我们不利的判决。

# interest

  1. current interest 活期利息
  2. These songs interest me. 这些歌曲引起了我的兴趣。
  3. conflict of interest 利益冲突

# entail

  1. The crisis would entail dissolution of the union. 这场危机必定会导致联盟的解体。
  2. His strategy does entail some risks. 用他的策略确实要承担一些风险。
  3. This job would entail knowing how to use a computer. 这项工作需要知道如何使用电脑。
